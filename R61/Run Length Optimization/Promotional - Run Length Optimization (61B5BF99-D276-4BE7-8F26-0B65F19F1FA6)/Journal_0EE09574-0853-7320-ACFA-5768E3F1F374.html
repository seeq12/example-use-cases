Run Length Optimization

 

Background: 

Determining if the decision to shut down and clean/regenerate equipment to regain throughput rate upon start-up allows a unit to meet their production goals sooner. 

Scenario: 

A manufacturing production unit needs to produce 50,000 klb of the product that they are running. 

The production rate for this product decreases at a relatively constant linear rate due to equipment fouling and plugging constraints. A 12-hour shutdown (production rate = 0 klb/h) can be taken at any point during a product run in order to recover production rate. 

Data: 

  • Production Rate

Date Range: 

  • Jan 1, 2020 12:00 AM - Mar 1, 2020 12:00 AM

Strategy:

To minimize the time taken to produce a given order quantity, you must maximize production rate over the long-term. You can assume that every production time period also has an associated downtime, but that the final (1) downtime does not impact order delivery date as it comes after the full order is produced.
