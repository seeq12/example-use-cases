Drift Detection and Modeling

Challenge

Meter based maintenance is costly and introduces additional failure risks in complex equipment. Even with maintenance, it is critical to monitor and detect early sign of failure to prevent significant impacts.

Autoclaves are a critical part of commercial GMP operations. Unplanned downtime can ripple through a plant.

Solution

Model the equipment's operations in real-time. Use the results to detect early signs of failure or drift. Reduce failures and make data-driven decisions.
