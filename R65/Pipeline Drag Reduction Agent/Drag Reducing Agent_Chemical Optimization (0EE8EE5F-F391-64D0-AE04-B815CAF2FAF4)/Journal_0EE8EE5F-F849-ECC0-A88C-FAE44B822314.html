Pipeline Drag Reducing Agent (DRA) Optimization

Problem Statement:

DRA injection is pivotal to midstream pipelines to ensure on specification product while reducing operating costs with transporting additional material. While its impact is undeniable, its high cost forces companies to optimize its usage to focus on problematic segments of their pipelines.

Solution:

Repeat the previous step, but this time aimed at segments where DRA injection can be reduced
