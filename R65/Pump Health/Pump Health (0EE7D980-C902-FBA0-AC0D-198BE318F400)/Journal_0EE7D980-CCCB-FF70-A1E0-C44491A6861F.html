Pump Health Monitoring

Problem Statement:

By identifying leading and lagging indicators of pump health and continuously monitor these indicator variables, poor performance can be detected and corrective actions can be taken.

Deviation from Pump Curve

Solution:

Access the raw data, display the pumps process signal.

Cleanse the raw signals by easily removing the downtime and outliers.

Identify when process data deviates +/- 30% from the design pump curve. Pump curves can be plotted using Seeq Add-on tool, Plot Digitizer
(Do it! Create a notification for when the pump is not in normal operation)

Identify the indicators, unique operating modes, based on deviation from the Best Efficiency Point (B.E.P. on reference image below). (all indicators)
