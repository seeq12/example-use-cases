CLPM can be used to identify the following controller related issues:

1. CO at a limit: may indicate process issue or mechanical sizing issue

- Scorecard shows FIC-020 AAE is increasing each week, while the CO travel decreases to 0%

- Trend of metrics

- Process data shows that CO is at 100%

2. Change in operating level: if a process is operating at a level that it was not designed / tuned for, performance issues my occur.

- Scorecard shows PIC-002 CO Travel and AAE are increasing.

- Trend of metrics

- Process data shows that the level of operation is changing over time

3. Wrong operating mode

- Scorecard shows TIC-001 AAE increases, CO Travel decreases and % Time Correct Mode decrease

- Trend of metrics

- Process data shows controller switched to Manual mode
