Polytropic Head

Determine the polytropic head

n => polytropic exponent

Za => average compressibility factor

R => real gas constant

MW => Molecular weight

T1 => suction temperature

P1 => suction pressure

P2 => discharge pressure


1. Determine the average compressibility factor

  • 1ST STAGE COMP SUCTION Z
  • 1ST STAGE COMP DISCHARGE Z
  • 1ST STAGE COMP Z_AVG

2. Determine the Molecular weight of the compressed gas.

Note: we are passing a temperature and pressure to the python script because it requires a full set of inputs even if not all inputs are required in the lookup. In reality, the MW is not affected by the thermodynamic state of the substance. 

3. Determine the polytropic head. 

Note1: It is very important to remember that pressures and temperatures are absolute in all calculations

Note2: The proper name is specific compression work but it is commonly referred to as polytropic head
