Polytropic Efficiency

Determine the polytropic efficiency 

1. We need to determine k which is the ratio of specific heats. Furthermore, K is the average value of k_suction and k_discharge since the properties of the gas are changing throughout the compression process. 

  • Calculate Cp and Cv both the suction and at the discharge (workstep- suction CP)
    • 1ST STAGE COMP SUCTION CP
    • 1ST STAGE COMP DISCHARGE CP
    • 1ST STAGE COMP SUCTION CV
    • 1ST STAGE COMP DISCHARGE CV
  • Calculate k values at the suction and discharge (workstep- suction K)
    • 1ST STAGE COMP DISCHARGE K
    • 1ST STAGE COMP SUCTION K
  • Calculate the average k across the compressor (1ST STAGE COMP K_AVG)
