Manufacturing OEE 

The Challenge

Combining, contextualizing, and taking action on disparate and dense data to optimized throughput.

The Impact

A top 5 pharmaceutical company used Seeq to monitor Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) finding $65M/year in additional production revenue.

The Solution 

Overall Equipment Effectiveness is a commonly used method to identify the top downtime, performance loss, and quality loss reasons. With Seeq OEE, inefficiencies are easily identified and improvement plans can begin immediately in real-time.
