Transformer Replacement Strategy using True Age Calculation

Outcome:

  • Reduces CapEx by prioritizing replacement based on actual equipment condition
  • Improve system reliability to avoid $10's of millions in unplanned downtime and CapEx and premature replacement

Challenge:

Most transformer manufacturers define the end of life as age 40, however ligher or more eavily loaded transformers can easily deviate from this value leading to unexpoected outages or replacement of transformers that still have substantail usable life left.
