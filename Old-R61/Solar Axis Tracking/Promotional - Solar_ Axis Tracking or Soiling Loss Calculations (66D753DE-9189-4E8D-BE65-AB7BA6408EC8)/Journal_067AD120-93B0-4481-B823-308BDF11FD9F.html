Maximizing Renewables Generation by Identifying System Losses


Summary

When Monitoring a solar plant, optimization of assets is vital because any loss in the system directly contributes to lost revenue, but also to a possible increase in net power emissions because the slack will need to be taken up, likely by an emitting thermal generation plant like a Gas Turrbine or a Coal Plant. Identifying losses related to sun axis tracking systems and panel soiling can help monitoring agencies or utlities to maximize generation from their PV assets, reducing the overall CO2 emitted per kW generated across their portfolio of assets. The Model of expected power output by the panel system generated in this usecase can then be utilized by monitoring agencies or utlities to get a real time estimate of the possible increase in CO2 emissions across their portfolio due to PV losses, and combine the emissions and lost revenue data to determine when or if maintenance should be performed to help the company or utility meet their sustainability goals
