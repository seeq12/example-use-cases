Pump Health Monitoring

Summary:

Identify leading and lagging indicators of pump health and continuously monitor these indicator variables to detect poor performance and take corrective actions.

Deviation from Pump Curve

Solution:

Access the raw data, display the pumps process signal.

Cleanse the raw signals by easily removing the downtime and outliers. (cleansed signals)

Utilized Seeq Add-on tool, Plot Digitizer, to plot the design pump curve. Identify the event of process data deviates +/- 30% from the design pump curve.

Identify the indicators, unique operating modes ,based on deviation from the Best Efficiency Point (B.E.P. on reference image below). (all indicators)

Quantify the total duration of each indicator on days of the year.

Monitor the pump indicators on daily basis. Ideally the pumps indicator should indicate 'Normal' (green) consistently and alert should be raised when other indicators start to appear. (Do it! Create a notification for when the pump is not in normal operation)
