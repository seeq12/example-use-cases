Pump Station Monitoring

Problem Statement

Sewer blockages release untreated waste into waterways, resulting in substantial environmental, monetary, and reputational consequences. 

Solution

With as little as 30 days of historical data, develop a profile of normal pump station behavior based on the day and time, and use this to monitor any deviations from the normal/acceptable profile.

1. Access raw data

2. Define peak operating times

3. Combine multiple periods of interest to identify weekend and weekday peak times.

4. Use point-and-click tools to develop a reference profile of pump station behavior during different times. (>capsule view) 

5. Monitor Deviations from the standard profile and monitor for issues across assets.
